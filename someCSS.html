Kangaroo Jack: A Cultural Icon

Without Kangaroo Jack, the knee-slapping outback based comedy released in 2003, children in the late nineties would have no idea how kangaroos act in the wild. Most people would assume they don't have human-like mannerisms at all, but this movie (based on a true story) proves that they could easily fit into human society if given the opportunity.
